使役动词 + 宾语 + 宾语补语


| 动词                                                                   | 结构                                  | 例句                                    | 含义            |
| -------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------- | ------------- |
| **make**                                                             | make + sb + V                       | She made me **cry**.                  | 使某人做（强迫）      |
| **let**                                                              | let + sb + V                        | My parents let me **go out**.         | 允许某人做         |
| **have**                                                             | have + sb + V                       | I had him **fix** my bike.            | 让某人做 / 请人做    |
| 这里的宾语可省略后，改写谓语                                                       | I had（him省略宾语） my car washed(过去分词). |                                       |               |
| **get**                                                              | get + sb + to V                     | I got him **to help** me.             | 设法让某人做（语气更委婉） |
| **help**                                                             | help + sb + (to) V                  | She helped me **(to) carry** the box. | 帮助做（to可省）     |
| **force**                                                            | force + sb + to V                   | They forced me **to sign**.           | 强迫做           |
| **allow / permit**                                                   | allow / permit + sb + to V          | They allowed me **to enter**.         | 允许做           |
| **encourage / remind / tell / ask / order / advise / invite / warn** | + sb + to V                         | He told me **to wait**.               | 各种“让某人去做”的动词  |
