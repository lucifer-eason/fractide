| **时态 / 体** | **常用标志**                                            | **例句**                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ---------- | --------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **过去时**    | yesterday, last …, ago, before, after               | 1. I **went** to the park **yesterday**.                              2. She **left** the office **before** lunch.                           3. They **arrived** **two days ago**                                   4. We **visited** London **last summer**. |
| **现在完成时**  | since, for, yet, already, just, recently, lately    | 1. I **have lived** here **since** 2015.                              2. He **has already finished** his homework.             3. We **haven’t seen** her **yet**.                                    4. She **has been very busy** **recently**.             |
| **进行时**    | now, at the moment, still, currently                | 1. She **is studying** **at the moment**.                          2. I **am still waiting** for the bus.                             3. They **are playing** football **now**.                            4. He **is currently working** on a new project.   |
| **完成进行时**  | for + 时间段, since + 起点, all day/week/month, how long | 1. I **have been working** here **for** 3 hours.                 2. She **has been waiting** **since** 9 am.                      3. They **have been playing** football **all afternoon**.  4. How long **have you been learning** English?                  |
| **将来时**    | tomorrow, next …, in + 将来时间, by + 将来时间              | 1. We **will meet** **tomorrow**.                                     2. I **will have finished** the report **by** Friday.           3. She **will start** school **next month**.                      4. They **will leave** **in two hours**.              |
