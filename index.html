Game Rules

Basic Gameplay

Fractide World is a fast-paced particle combat game where players control energy fragments in an arena. Your goal is to absorb smaller particles while avoiding larger threats.

Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys to move
  • Space to dash/boost
  • Mouse to aim special abilities
  • Q/E to cycle through abilities

Scoring

Points are awarded for absorbing particles (+1 to +100 based on size) and defeating opponents (+500). The larger you grow, the more points you're worth to others.

Power-ups

Random power-ups spawn in the arena, granting temporary abilities like speed boosts, shields, or area attacks.
